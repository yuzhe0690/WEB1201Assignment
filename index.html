Globally

  • Globally, depression has affected around 280 million people, approximately 3.8% of the world's population (World Health Organization, 2021).
    It is also accountable for the overall burden of disease globally (Hope For Depression, n.a.).
  • It causes suicide and it is one of the few main causes that are accountable for deaths of 15-29 years old.
    Each year there are more than 700 thousand people suicide from depression (WHO, 2021).
  • People that are in the low and middle income level countries received no treatment for depression even though there are effective treatments to cure depression.
    The percentage of people not receiving treatment has already exceeeded 75% (WHO, 2021).
